Frequently Asked Questions

How is company size determined?

Company size is based on annual revenue. We trust our customers to self-select the appropriate tier: Indie (under $100K), Studio ($100K-$1M), or Enterprise ($1M+). For studios with variable revenue, we're happy to discuss the best fit.

Can I upgrade from Annual to Perpetual?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time. We'll credit a portion of your annual payments toward the perpetual license.

What's included in integration support?

We help you integrate RVR into your project and CI/CD pipeline. This includes setup guidance, troubleshooting, and best practices for your specific use case.

Is the license per-seat or per-company?

Per-company. One license covers your entire team - no per-seat fees, no counting developers.

What happens when a new Unreal Engine version releases?

Annual license holders receive engine upgrades automatically at no extra cost. Perpetual license holders can purchase upgrade packages or handle upgrades themselves with source access.
