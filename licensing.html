Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Standard license?

The Standard license is a one-time purchase that gives you the compiled RVR plugin. You get all recording features, all platforms, and offline recording. No source code is included. Pay once, receive your API key, and start recording.

How does the subscription work?

Choose monthly or yearly, pay via Stripe, and receive your API key by email. Enter the key in your project settings and start recording. Your subscription must remain active to keep recording — internet connection is required. Engine upgrades are included. Cancel anytime.

What is the Perpetual license?

The Perpetual license is a one-time purchase with full source code, custom engine support, offline recording, and priority support with consultation calls. Pricing is by request only — contact us for a custom quote tailored to your studio's needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Chinese users can pay with Alipay — it appears automatically at checkout. All transactions are processed in USD.

How do I receive my API key?

After payment is confirmed, an API key is sent to the email you used at checkout. Enter the key in Unreal Engine under Project Settings → Plugins → Runtime Video Recorder, and you're ready to record.

Can I upgrade from Standard or Subscription to Perpetual?

Yes. Contact us and we'll help you transition. We can credit your previous purchase toward the perpetual license price.

What happens when a new Unreal Engine version releases?

Subscribers receive engine upgrades automatically at no extra cost. Standard license holders can purchase upgrade packages. Perpetual license holders handle upgrades themselves with their source code access.

Is the license per-seat or per-company?

The Perpetual license is per-company — one license covers your entire team. Standard and Subscription licenses are per-seat/project as defined in the license terms.
