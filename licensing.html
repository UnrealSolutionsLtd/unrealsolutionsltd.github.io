Frequently Asked Questions

How does the subscription work?

Subscribe to a monthly or yearly plan, connect your API key, and start recording. Your subscription stays active as long as you're subscribed. Cancel anytime.

What's the difference between a subscription and a company license?

Subscriptions are self-serve and instant — subscribe and start recording. Company licenses are for studios that need source code access, offline recording, dedicated support, custom SLAs, or perpetual ownership.

Can I upgrade from a subscription to a company license?

Yes. Contact us and we'll help you transition to a company license that fits your needs.

Is the company license per-seat or per-company?

Per-company. One license covers your entire team — no per-seat fees, no counting developers.

What happens when a new Unreal Engine version releases?

Subscribers and annual license holders receive engine upgrades automatically at no extra cost. Perpetual license holders can purchase upgrade packages or handle upgrades themselves with source access.
