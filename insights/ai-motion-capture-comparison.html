Best Mocap Workflow for Unreal Engine 5

Capture Reference Video

Record your actor with good lighting, minimal occlusion, and camera at waist height. For multi-view setups (Move AI), position cameras at 45¬∞ intervals.

Pro tip: Use 60fps minimum for fast movements. Solid contrasting background (green screen not required) improves tracking accuracy by 15-20%.

Process Through AI Mocap Tool

Upload to your chosen platform. Configure skeleton type (usually "Unreal Mannequin" or "Mixamo") for optimal retargeting later.

Export settings: FBX with baked keyframes, 30fps for cinematics, 60fps for gameplay animations. Enable root motion if your game uses it.

Import to Unreal Engine

Import FBX with "Import Animations" enabled. Use UE5's IK Retargeter to map the animation to your game skeleton.

IK Retargeter setup: Create IK Rig for both source skeleton (AI mocap output) and target skeleton (your character). Map bone chains, then create IK Retargeter asset.

Polish in Control Rig

Use UE5's Control Rig for final adjustments. Fix foot sliding, smooth transitions, and add secondary motion.

Common fixes: Ground contact (Two Bone IK solve), hand positions (FK adjustments), hip height correction (procedural offset).

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does AI motion capture cost in 2025?

AI motion capture tools now start at $15/month for basic plans. DeepMotion offers plans from $15/month, Plask starts at $20/month, and Move AI begins at $29/month. Free options like Rokoko Vision offer limited credits. Enterprise pricing typically runs $200-500/month for unlimited processing.

Which AI mocap tool is best for Unreal Engine integration?

Move AI and DeepMotion offer the best direct Unreal Engine integration with FBX export and retargeting support. Both support UE5's Control Rig and IK Retargeter. Plask requires additional processing for optimal UE5 results but offers excellent web-based previews.

Is AI motion capture accurate enough for AAA games?

AI mocap achieves 85-95% accuracy of traditional marker-based systems for most body movements. It excels at locomotion, combat, and dialogue scenes. However, precise finger tracking and subtle facial expressions still benefit from traditional mocap or manual polish for AAA quality standards.

What is markerless motion capture?

Markerless motion capture uses AI and computer vision to track human movement from regular video without requiring special suits, markers, or dedicated hardware. Tools like DeepMotion, Move AI, and Plask can extract animation data from smartphone or webcam footage.

Can I use AI motion capture for commercial game projects?

Yes, all major AI mocap platforms (DeepMotion, Move AI, Plask) include commercial usage rights in their paid plans. Free tiers typically have restrictions, so check licensing terms before using in released games. Most platforms require attribution only for free-tier usage.
