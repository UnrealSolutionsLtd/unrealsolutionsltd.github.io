Frequently Asked Questions

How does video to game-ready animation work?

Our AI mocap pipeline extracts motion data from any video source using markerless pose estimation. The AI then automatically cleans up noise, fixes foot sliding, and retargets the animation to your character rig. Export directly to Unreal Engine with one click.

What makes this different from traditional mocap workflows?

Traditional mocap requires expensive suits, markers, and hours of cleanup work. Our AI pipeline uses markerless capture from any video‚Äîphone recordings, reference footage, even YouTube clips. AI handles cleanup automatically, reducing days of work to minutes.

Can indie studios afford AI mocap for their games?

Yes! Our pipeline eliminates the need for expensive mocap studios and lengthy cleanup processes. Indie teams can capture performances anywhere with just a camera, then use AI to produce production-quality animations at a fraction of traditional costs.

How do I integrate AI mocap animations into Unreal Engine 5?

Our one-click export creates UE5-ready animation assets. The retargeting system automatically maps animations to your MetaHuman or custom skeleton. Import directly into Sequencer for cinematics or use in gameplay animation blueprints.

Is this suitable for game trailers and cutscenes?

Absolutely. The AI cleanup produces cinematic-quality animations. Many studios use our pipeline for game trailers, in-engine cutscenes, and promotional content. The quality matches traditional mocap at a fraction of the time and cost.

What video quality do I need for good results?

The AI works with a wide range of video quality. For best results, we recommend at least 720p resolution at 30fps. Good lighting and a clear view of the performer's full body will produce the highest quality motion data.

What Unreal Engine versions are supported?

Unreal Engine 5+ only. The AI Mocap Pipeline exports directly to UE5-compatible formats and integrates with Sequencer and Animation Blueprints.
