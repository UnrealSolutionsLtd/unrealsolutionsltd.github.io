Who Uses BugIt?

Built for QA teams and developers who are tired of "cannot reproduce".

QA Teams

Manual Testing

Stop writing long bug reports. Press one button when you see a bug - BugIt captures video proof, your exact inputs, and all the logs. Attach the link to your ticket and move on.

  • One-button bug capture
  • Video evidence for every bug
  • No more typing repro steps

Developers

Bug Fixing

No more back-and-forth asking for repro steps. Open BugIt, watch the video, see exactly what inputs triggered the bug, check the logs at that moment. Fix it.

  • Reproduce on first try
  • See inputs + logs + video together
  • Identify performance issues instantly
