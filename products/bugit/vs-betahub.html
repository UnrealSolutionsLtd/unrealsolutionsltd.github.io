Key Differences Explained

üéØ Frame-Synced Evidence

BugIt doesn't just collect videos and logs separately - it synchronizes everything to the exact frame. When you scrub through the video, you see:

  • Exactly which buttons were pressed at that moment
  • The log entries from that exact frame
  • Frame timing to identify if there was a performance spike

This is what makes "cannot reproduce" bugs reproducible on the first try.

üîí Self-Hosted

BugIt runs entirely on your infrastructure. Your bug reports, videos, and player data never leave your servers. This matters for:

  • Studios with security/compliance requirements
  • Console development under NDA
  • Air-gapped or offline environments
  • Teams that prefer full control over their data

üì± All Platforms Including Mobile

BugIt (powered by RVR) records natively on every platform:

  • Desktop: Windows, macOS, Linux
  • Mobile: Android, iOS
  • VR: Quest, PCVR
  • Consoles: PlayStation, Xbox, Switch (add-on)

No external screen recording apps, no performance overhead from capture software - just in-engine recording that works everywhere.

The Bottom Line

BugIt is built for internal QA teams that need frame-synced video evidence to reproduce bugs on the first try. Self-hosted, open source, and works on every platform including mobile.

If you need your data on your servers and want to eliminate "cannot reproduce" forever, BugIt is for you.
