Privacy Policy

About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Unreal Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Runtime Video Audio Recorder (RVR) plugin and related services such Technical Demo applications based on RVR. This policy complies with VRC.Quest.Privacy.1 standards and other applicable privacy regulations.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Email address, name, and company information when you contact us for support or licensing
  • Personal Content: Videos recorded by RVR plugin for immediate playback within the application

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our plugin performance
  • Technical Data: Device information, operating system, Unreal Engine version, and plugin version
  • Website Analytics: Google Analytics data including page views, referral sources, and user behavior
  • Error Reports: Crash reports and error logs to improve stability (with your consent)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Content: We do not access, store, or transmit your recorded videos, audio, or project files
  • Biometric Data: No facial recognition, voice patterns, or biometric information is collected
  • Location Data: We do not track your physical location
  • Social Media Data: We do not access your social media accounts or contacts

How We Use Your Information

  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety

Data Security

We implement standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All video recordings are stored only on your device and can easily be removed via Oculus Gallery

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your information
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Active Users: While your account is active and for a reasonable period after
  • Support Records: For the duration of support and a reasonable period after
  • Legal Requirements: As required by applicable laws and regulations

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
