### RVR Competitive Advantages

1. **No FFmpeg dependency** - Native OS APIs
2. **Hardware acceleration** - WMF, AVFoundation, MediaCodec
3. **Multi-platform** - Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, Oculus
4. **Zero game thread blocking** - Async encoding
5. **VR support** - Unique in marketplace
6. **Circular buffer** - Instant replay capability
7. **CI/CD ready** - Headless operation
