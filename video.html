About Runtime Video Audio Recorder (RVR)

RVR is a cutting-edge video and audio recording solution designed specifically for Unreal Engine developers. It provides high-quality .MP4 video capture with synchronized sound across multiple platforms including Windows, MacOS, Oculus, Android, and Linux.

Key Features:

  • Render Target Recording for VR games and specialized capture scenarios
  • Camera Recording with full support for standard and Cinematic cameras
  • High-quality Audio Recording on all platforms (except Linux)
  • Offscreen Recording including audio
  • Flexible Frame Rate - record at 60 FPS even when VR game runs at 30 FPS
  • Cross-Platform Support across Windows, MacOS, Oculus, Android, and Linux
  • Smart Viewport Recording with automatic frame detection
  • Hardware-accelerated video encoding via WMF/MediaCodec
  • Compatible with all major Unreal Engine versions

Technical Advantages:

  • No ffmpeg dependency - superior performance and reliability
  • Hardware acceleration via OS-provided APIs
  • Maintains peak game performance while recording
  • Active Discord community for support
  • Trusted by leading companies worldwide including Amazon Games, Meta Research, and more
